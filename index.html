The shared operating system (every doc applies this)

  • Variety IS the targeting. Andromeda ranks at the creative level — fill the Persona × Awareness matrix instead of chasing one winner.
  • Under-exploited stages. Across these accounts the cheapest untapped market is Unaware / Problem Aware — most competitors only write Product/Most Aware.
  • One format per week (rotate monthly), 3–5 matrix cells, one ad per cell, change multiple signals to beat similarity clustering.
  • Judge on CPA + AOV, promote winning post IDs to the scaling campaign (2-Campaign Method). Win = 3–5 sales or 3–10 booked calls per concept.
  • Reference: One-Page SOP

⚠ Flags & research gaps (read before scaling)

  • Gated landing pages: several LPs (CEO Lawyer, Quintessa, VAM, Trusy) were JS/bot-gated during research — offers were reconstructed from local briefs/assets. Verify exact price, guarantee wording, and CTA against the live page before launch.
  • Unconfirmed figures: prices and stat claims (e.g. VAM "from $1,597", Nurse Coach ~$1,500 CPA, Trusy tiers) came from mined docs, not live confirmation — treat as draft until a media buyer confirms.
  • No performance data: docs are built from offer/angle research, not ad-account results. First-week batches are hypotheses; re-weight the portfolio once real CPA/ROAS lands.
  • Compliance: Dan Henry (income-claim disclaimers), legal accounts (no outcome guarantees) — guardrails are in each doc's Compliance section.
